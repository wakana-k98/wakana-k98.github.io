About me

Where design

I'm studying Cognitive Systems (Cognition & Brain) at UBC — a degree at the intersection of neuroscience, computer science, and design. That blend shapes how I approach every project: always thinking about the human on the other side of the screen.

I've built mobile apps, web platforms, and embedded systems. I've won hackathon awards for accessibility and wellness design. I research machine learning and computational neuroscience. Trilingual in English, Japanese, and French.

I'm drawn to work that makes something complex feel effortless, and something lonely feel a little less so.
