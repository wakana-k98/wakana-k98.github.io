About me

I'm a third-year Cognitive Systems student at UBC, studying the intersection of neuroscience, computer science, and human-centred design. That background shapes everything I build. I'm always thinking about the person on the other side of the screen.

My goal is to create things that are not just functional, but genuinely intuitive, inclusive, and meaningful. I care deeply about the details that make a product feel thoughtful rather than just built.

Currently exploring roles in full-stack development, UX/product design, and HCI research. I love working at the intersection of all three.
