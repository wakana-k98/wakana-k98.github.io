About me

Building at the edge of
design & code.

I'm a Cognitive Systems student at UBC, studying the intersection of neuroscience, computer science, and human-centred design. That background shapes everything I build. I'm always thinking about the person on the other side of the screen.

My goal is to create digital products that are not just functional, but genuinely intuitive, inclusive, and meaningful. Whether that's an app tackling student loneliness, an accessibility-first dining platform, or a voice-first tool for seniors with memory loss, I bring the same care to all of it.

Currently exploring roles in full-stack development, UX/product design, and HCI research. I love working at the intersection of all three.
